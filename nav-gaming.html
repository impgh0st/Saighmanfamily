Fallout 76

Fallout is a series of games that started in the late 1990's and revolves around a single player that lived in an underground vault after the events of WW3. The protagonist wanders the surface of a post nuculear war to find a way to survive. Through the journey, they find horrifying information on the company that creates the vaults, and possibly instigated the global war.

Fallout 76 was the first attempt by Bethesda Softworks to create a multiplayer approach to the game, and create a world that incorporates group progression, and teamwork. The characters that are played in the game are the first generation of Vault Dwellers to surface after 25 years of isolation. They are tasked with finding the Overseer of the vault and to establish a new form of government and currency. Through their voyage they encounter various other factions; Raiders, Brotherhood of Steel (the former US Army), The Foundation, The Responders, and the Enclave (Reminants of the former US Government). Each of these factions attempt to take on the taks of government individually, but have all failed. With the help of the Vault Dweller, these factions are brought together to create a functioning society in the mountains of Appalachia. Specifically, West Virginia.



Halo: Infinite

This expansion is the 6th game in the main series of Halo, and continues the story after the events of Halo 5. The story follows the protagonist, Spartan 117, The Master Chief. A genetically and physically altered super soldier that was inteded to stop an insurrection in the United Planetary Aliance founded by the UNSC. However, before the insurrection could be stopped, the UNSC was attacked by a conglomerate of alien species known as the Covenant. The Covenant had the belief that they are the ancestors of a race called the forerunners, and that they had the right to concur the galaxy in their name.

As the story begins in Infinite, Master Chief is defeated by a brute war chieftan named, and is left for dead along with every other member of the crew on a space cruiser called the Infinate. One other survivor finds Chief and rescues him with the hope of returning to Earth. However, Chief recieved a message from a ring like structure and tells his rescuer to land on the ring. He finds himself back in a hopeless war agains the Banished, which is the last remaining cell of the Covenant. Can the Master Chief find a way to survive and stop the war>



Generation Zero

Generation Zero is a game that we came across at the suggestion of our second eldest. The premise of the game is set in a post appocyliptic Sweeden, that is ridden with hostile machines. The machines were created by a company with the intent to create autonomus soldiers. However the mainframe of the machines is taken over and controled by a hostile force that intends to eradicate humanity. The protagonist is a young adult that is a member of the military returning from a camping trip with a friend. When you arrive at the dock, you find your frinds body, dead and the boat used destroyed. Your goal is to surive.



Tiny Tina's Wonderlands

This game is a unique spin on a series of games called Borderlands. The expansion is from the mind of a tween named Tiny Tina, who is a Bunker Master. The idea of the game is a pseudo version of Dungeons and Dragons but uses modernized weapons. You play as a player character in a story created by Tina. Your goal is to save the Queen of the land and defeat the Dragon Lord. As you progress through the story, you create a unique character by assigning stat points, bonus skills and finding the weapons and armor that meet the needs of your character. Once you finish the game, the end game content is a crucible of extra levels and advanced gear.



Diablo III: Reaper of Souls

Diablo III is a game that has been around since 2012, and continues to recieve new updates, game modes, and customizations. The popularity for the game is slowly diminishing as the content is stale and hasn't really improved much. However, with the announcement of Diablo 4, release date is June 6, 2023, gamers have been returning to this masterpiece to refresh their knowledge of lore and gameplay. The story is about a hero born of both angelic and human dna, called a Nephilim. The character is defending humanity from a war between heaven and hell, with earth caught in the middle as the battle grounds. Your goal is to stop the war, find the ultimate gear and compete in seasonal activities.
